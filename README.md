Project Overview 
This project highlights the planning, creation, and analysis of a digital marketing campaign built for Iron Pulse Fitness Studio. The main goal of the campaign was to use Facebook and Instagram ads to find high-quality local leads and increase gym memberships. Operating with a practical budget of ₹5,000 over a 6-day test phase, the campaign successfully generated 150 verified customer leads.

Target Audience
StrategyTo save money and avoid wasting ad budget on people living too far away, the campaign targeted individuals within a strict 5-kilometre radius of the gym. The ads were shown to men and women between the ages of 18 and 30 who showed active interests in physical fitness, gymnasiums, weight loss, healthy diets, and competitor fitness brands like Gold's Gym and Cult.fit.

Ad Creative and Copywriting
The campaign used a clean, high-impact single-image ad featuring a strong visual hook: "FREE 3-DAY TRIAL ACCESS PASS + COMPLIMENTARY DIET CHART." The ad text used the classic marketing framework to grab local attention, build interest around premium equipment and expert trainers, and create urgency by limiting the offer to the first 50 people.

Lead Funnel Design
Instead of sending people to an external website where they might leave without signing up, the campaign used Meta Instant Forms directly inside Facebook and Instagram. The form smoothly collected the user's name, email, and address. To ensure the leads were high quality and that fake phone numbers were blocked, an advanced SMS verification step was added, requiring users to enter a 4-digit code sent to their phone before submitting.

Campaign Results
The campaign performed exceptionally well and achieved highly cost-effective numbers:
Total Impressions: The gym ad appeared on local phone screens 50,000 times.
Click-Through Rate (CTR): A strong 2.50% of people clicked on the ad graphic, showing the design was highly attractive.
Total Clicks: The ad received 1,250 total clicks.
Leads Generated: 150 real local phone numbers were successfully captured for the gym's database.
Cost Per Lead (CPL): Each verified lead cost only ₹33.33, making it a highly profitable strategy for a small business.

Key Marketing Takeaways
Low Friction Wins: Keeping users inside the Facebook app using Instant Forms keeps sign-up rates much higher than sending them to slow external websites.
Free Trials Outperform Discounts: Offering a free experience (like a 3-day pass) is much more attractive to local customers than standard membership discounts.
Quality Over Quantity: Adding an SMS phone verification step ensures that the final list of leads consists only of real, interested people, saving the sales team from calling fake numbers.
